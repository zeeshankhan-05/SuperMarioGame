Super Mario: Rescue Princess Peach (from what? I don't even know)
Embark on a classic adventure to save Princess Peach from the mysterious danger awaiting her! Dodge the pesky Koopa Troopas and mischievous Goombas, and power up with iconic items like the Super Mushroom and Fire Flower to become invincible.

Controls
Move Right: Right Arrow
Move Left: Left Arrow
Crouch (Big Mario only): Down Arrow
Jump: Press "S"
Sprint: Hold "A"
Shoot Fireballs (Fire Mario only): Tap "A"
Power up, avoid enemies, and dash through levels to complete your mission!
