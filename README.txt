Save Princess Peach (from what? I don't even know...) by passing the Koopa Troopas and Goombas with powerups such as big mushrooms and fire flowers.

Mario Controls:

Right arrow - move right
Left arrow - move left
Down arrow - crouch (only avaliable with Big Mario)
"s" - jump
hold "a" - sprint
tap "a" - shoot fire (only avaliable with Fire Mario)
